One gripe with the comic is how major developments happen off-screen. Being able to kill the Watcher is a monumental, but the author resorts to handwaving Mr. Fantastic’s ingenuity. Similar thing occurs with Dr. Brighton affecting Deadpool’s mind through an app on his tablet :-/

SPIDERMAN. You’re lucky I’m not like you. You’re lucky I don’t kill you for what you’ve done.
Am I? Do you think they would let you break character even if you wanted to?
