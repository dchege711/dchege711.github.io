Fiction

Dated Jul 25, 2021; last modified on Sun, 11 Sep 2022

Especially interested in fiction that addresses grand themes, e.g. humanity’s place in the universe, justice, etc. Although fiction writers don’t aim to be as thorough as academics, they still pose stimulating hypotheses.

Snapshots of noteworthy moments. Tired of mumbling my way through questions like, “What did you like about The Sandman?”

Worlds Without Ends and Will Eisner’s Comic Industry Awards are good resources of renown fiction.
