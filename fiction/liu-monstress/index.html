Plot Points

[Maika] Go on, all of you. RAGE. Madness just struck your friends and loved ones, and probably had to kill them to survive. But none of you can deal with that, can you? You’d rather blame someone else, instead of feeling your grief. You’d rather keep killing instead of remembering. You’re murderers. But worse, you are cowards.

[Maika] The cumaea don’t think we’re people either, old man. They say we don’t have souls. [Lord Doctor] We can quibble ethics all day, daughter, but there can be no denying that we are on the brink of war. A war that will be decided by intelligence. I do not intend to lose that war because of a frailty of conscience.

[Maika] I felt the same last night after I discovered we’d killed. Horrified… But not suicidal. That terrifies me… More than anything. It means I’m okay with killing… and eating… so I can survive.

[Crow] I was raised to believe one must sacrifice the few if it saves the many. But how many? How few? Would you sacrifice Kippa to save the world? I don’t believe it would even cross your mind. And that, Lady Halfwolf, is the answer to the question. You fight for a who… not a what.
