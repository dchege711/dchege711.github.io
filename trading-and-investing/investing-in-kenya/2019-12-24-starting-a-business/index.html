Starting a Business in Kenya

Dated Dec 24, 2019; last modified on Sat, 25 Apr 2020

http://www.invest.go.ke/ has 3 components:

  • A database on Vision 2030 projects and mega-investment opportunities
  • An investor guide: labor matters, taxes, investor rights, etc.
  • eRegulations platform: setting up and running businesses.

Many steps need to be completed in Kenya. Appoint an agent if need be.

Types of business vehicles: sole proprietorship, company, foreign company with local branch, LLP, partnership
