It’s All About Real Estate

I admit that I’m biased towards the conclusion that the Kenyan real estate market is in a bubble. My ‘evidence’ is the notion that people are buying land, holding onto it as it appreciates, then selling it off. Reminds me of Malkiel’s discussion on how the crowd can get uber-speculative, abandoning reality itself

Questions to Dig Into

  • Who is in real estate? Who are the main players?
  • Why the craze for real estate?
    • Are they even flocking, or is it an exagerration by the media and my biased sample?
    • What’s the market size?
    • What’s the return?
  • Are the prices fair? There are a couple of portals to identify real estate opportunities: housejumia.co.ke , buyrentkenya.com , propertyleo.com , kenyareal.com , property24.co.ke , etc.
