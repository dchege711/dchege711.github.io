[Summary] (Sivan) Innovation Africa & Water Availability

Dated Oct 1, 2018; last modified on Mon, 27 Jan 2020

Innovation Africa & Water Availability. Sivan (InnoAfrica). Talks at The Keller Center. 2018.

Bring in Israeli solar panels and drip irrigation. Pump water from the large reservoirs below. Cost $7,000.

Adapting to community: 6-8 weeks on committees, bank accounts; mobile charging stations and barbershops using solar panels for sustainability. Can't sell the water, but you can subsidize it with complementary products.

Set up remote monitoring with real time stats in order to schedule maintenance.
