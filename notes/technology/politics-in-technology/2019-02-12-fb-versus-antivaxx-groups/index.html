FB Under Pressure to Halt Rise of Anti-Vaccination Groups [The Guardian]

Dated Feb 12, 2019; last modified on Mon, 27 Jan 2020

FB Under Pressure to Halt Rise of Anti-Vaccination Groups. Ed Pilkington, Jessica Glenza. The Guardian. https://www.theguardian.com/technology/2019/feb/12/facebook-anti-vaxxer-vaccination-groups-pressure-misinformation. 2019-02-12.

Vitamin C & Orthomolecular Medicine for Optimal Health. Closed Group. 49,000 approved members.

  • Gironda, an admin, sells high-dose Vitamin C in bulk through Revitalize Wellness. The ‘shop now’ button links to her firm.
  • Revitalize Wellness carries a disclaimer saying that its products are “not intended to treat, diagnose, cure or prevent disease”.
  • Gironda in the FB group: Vitamin C has an amazing record of fighting the same diseases vaccines were made for… I think the cons outweigh the pros on vaccines… Through greed they became a weapon. Until they become safe and not driven by money I would avoid all vaccines.

In discussions on the closed groups, members frequently express incorrect information that is reinforced by their peers.

  • Woman living near a measles outbreak: What should I be upping for my kids to keep them better protected?
    • “Load up on vitamin A. Measles susceptibility skyrockets among people with low vitamin A.”

Facebook has taken in ad $ from groups that specifically target parents with often frightening false messages meant to undermine trust in vaccines.

We don’t let big pharma or big food or big radio companies do this, so why should we let this happen in this FB?
