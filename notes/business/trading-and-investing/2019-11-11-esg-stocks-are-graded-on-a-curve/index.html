BlackRock designs its sustainable funds to match risk/return of broad market indexes while including the highest ESG-rated companies in each sector - the grading is on a curve.

8/10 of the biggest U.S. sustainable funds are invested in oil-and-gas companies (wsj.com).

The goal is not to pick environmentally conscious companies; the goal is to track the S&P 500 while having ‘ESG’ in the name.
