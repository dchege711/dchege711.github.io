ESG! The Meme [Epsilon Theory]

Dated Jan 29, 2019; last modified on Sun, 12 Apr 2020

Kobayashi Maru. Epsilon Theory. https://www.epsilontheory.com/kobayashi-maru/. Jan 29, 2019.

Environmental, Social and Governance Investing. So general as to be meaningless: marketing BS (thinking ‘holistically’ about ESG risk factors as part of the investment process) to impact investing (providing wholesale financing for community lending initiatives).

ESG! The meme is about selling portfolios as virtue signaling devices. It moves the conversation away from fees and performance to values. Don't you want to make a difference?
