The Trajectory of Great Ideas [Morgan Housel]

Dated Aug 14, 2018; last modified on Sun, 12 Apr 2020

The Trajectory of Great Ideas. Morgan Housel. Collaborative Fund. https://www.collaborativefund.com/blog/ideas/. Aug 14, 2018.

Sears. Blockbuster. Nokia. AOL. You had a great idea. You discovered a beautiful beach to lay on, but other people didn't just follow you and lay down themselves; they built condos and restaurants while you were watching the sunset. Soon your great idea loses its appeal.

But you put so much work into the great idea that it's become an inseparable part of your identity. Then the world changed - as it always does - but you kept that mentality of conviction and defense. Patience has turned to stubbornness. And now you're the proud owner of a bad idea.
