The Beginner's Mind

Being right is the enemy of staying right because it leads you to forget the way the world works.

Knowing you have a competitive advantage reduces the incentive to explore other ideas, especially when those ideas conflict with your chosen strategy.

The Burden of Being Profitable

Nothing to lose is a wonderful thing to have. Having a quarterly dividend to maintain is what happens after you build something great. But it can come at the expense of what made you successful in the first place.
