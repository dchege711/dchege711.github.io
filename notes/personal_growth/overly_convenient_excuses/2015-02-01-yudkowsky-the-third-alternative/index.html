[Summary] (Yudkowsky) The Third Alternative

Dated Feb 1, 2015; last modified on Sat, 21 Mar 2020

The Third Alternative. Eliezer Yudkowsky. Rationality: From AI to Zombies. Feb 2015.

If Santa-belief is destroyed by truth, the children will lose their sense of wonder and stop behaving nicely. It does not follow that Santa-ism is the best of all possible alternatives.

People justify questionable policy by pointing to a supposed benefit over the null action. The search for alternatives can go on forever, so one needs a stopping criterion. However, sometimes our conscious motives for the search don't square up with our subconscious influences, e.g. Searching for altruistic alternatives, but settling for one that's not extremely altruistic, but has some personal convenience.
