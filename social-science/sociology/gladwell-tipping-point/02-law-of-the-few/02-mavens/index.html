2. Mavens

Dated Jul 8, 2016; last modified on Sun, 18 Sep 2022

While Connectors connect us with new people, Mavens connect us with information.

Mavens notice the details, e.g. connecting Brazilian frost to coffee prices and stockpiling in anticipation, noticing when a supermarket slaps an “everyday low price!” without actually lowering the price, etc.

A Connector might tell 10 friends where to stay in LA and 5 might take his advice. A Maven might tell 5 people where to stay in LA but make the case so emphatically that all 5 would take the advice.
