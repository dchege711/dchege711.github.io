The Wolf that Tried to Play Nice

 

AT&T restricted FaceTime from running on some data plans. Justifications?

 

  • FaceTime is preloaded, therefore 100% of users have it
  • FaceTime wasn't efficient in network usage.
  • Then capacity was asymmetrical, but video chat is symmetrical

 

Interestingly enough, AT&T required Apple to greenlight it, which they did. AT&T worked with top developers to teach network etiquette.
