How to Study [Book]

Dated Jan 1, 1917; last modified on Sat, 06 Jun 2020

How to Study. Swain, George Fillmore. Jan 1, 1917.

Dad got us a kindle when I was in high school. This book was on it, and so I read all of it. I don’t think I’d have stumbled on it in the wild.

Given that it was written in 1917, it should be interesting to see what still holds.
