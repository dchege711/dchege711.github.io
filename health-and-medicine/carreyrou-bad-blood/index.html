Bad Blood: Secrets and Lies in a Silicon Valley Startup. Carreyrou, John. May 21, 2018. ISBN: 9781524731663.

Theranos shocked me. Before joining uni, Holmes was a role model to me - she was going to make it big despite being so young. Part of me wants to understand that she wasn’t a villain through and through. Maybe she was an inventor who built magnificent castles on a foundation of sand.
